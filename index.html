Noah's #75Hard Challenge!

For 75 days I must:

  1. Be on a Strict Diet

    • 3 Meals daily, with 2 hours between each.

    • Limited added sugars (<100% daily).

      • Natural sugars found in things like fruit are okay.

    • No alcohol.

  2. Have Two Workouts per Day

    • Each workout must be at least 45 minutes.

    • One workout must be outside.

    • Workouts must be separated by at least 3 hours.

    • Do 3 pull-ups whenever going downstairs

      • This one doesn't count towards workouts.

  3. Drink One Gallon of Water

    • No flavorings, or substitutions with other liquids.

  4. Commit Something via Github to this Page Every Day.

    • Contributions to Noahsnook.me also count once this page is moved there.

    • Github contributions must be green for 75 days straight.

  5. Take a Progress Picture
